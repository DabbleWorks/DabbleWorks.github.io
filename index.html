Hi there, and welcome to the Dabble Works! We make games and like to like to talk a lot about the process of designing games. There are some older free games here, and we're dipping our toes into commercial games. Check out our library of games below:

Paper Robot

Follow the adventures of the recently reactivated robot D4-BB13 (or Dabble for short) as he travels near and far in his quest for the golden gears needed to repair the legendary Dream Machine. Meet a huge cast of colorful characters, and team up with new partners to explore the lands. Collect modules and items to power up Dabble in this classic turn-based, low-number combat system where every point of damage counts!

Pen Pals

Things are going HAYwire down at the farm! Corral a herd of particularly round barnyard friends back into the pen. When two matching animals bump into each other, you'll find an even larger animal in their place. Things get crowded quickly, so get a mooove on in Pen Pals!
