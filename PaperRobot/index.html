Paper Robot

Follow the adventures of the recently reactivated robot D4-BB13 (or Dabble for short) as he travels near and far in his quest for the golden gears needed to repair the legendary Dream Machine. Meet a huge cast of colorful characters, and team up with new partners to explore the lands. Collect modules and items to power up Dabble in this classic turn-based, low-number combat system where every point of damage counts!


This game is in active development. As of late 2024 we are targeting a demo release for Summer 2025, and (optimistically) a full release in 2029.

Interested in seeing more? This game's development is live-streamed at 8 PM Eastern on Sunday, Monday, Wednesday, and Friday.
